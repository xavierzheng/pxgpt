   - Root characteristics (density, structure, length, root hair, color, morphology)

5. Before drawing conclusions, ensure:
   - All key identifying features have been examined
   - Lighting effects are considered
   - You've checked for conflicting evidence

Work through the aspects above carefully and systematically, then write a single comprehensive report describing the plant morphology in full detail.
